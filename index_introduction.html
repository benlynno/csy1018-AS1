Biography of Ekow Bentil

My name is Ekow Bentil, a first year BEng Computing (Computer Network Engineering) student and a resident of Northampton. I am studying Computer Networks Engineering because I trust this course will provide the platform for me to progress into a career with awesome potential. I am a hardworking, disciplined, trustworthy, ambitious man with impeccable time management and an excellent team worker. Before my I enrolled on this course, I was analytical logistics specialist in the HM Forces for nine years. During my years in the service, I deployed to many countries including Afghan, Canada, Falkland Islands, Germany and Cyprus. Yea, that is all you need to know about me, really.
